**Quality criteria** (Bold + Premium thumbnail aesthetic):

**1. SCROLL-STOPPING POWER (0-3 points)** 🎯 MOST IMPORTANT
- Bold, high-contrast colors that POP — this is non-negotiable
- Clear visual hierarchy — one obvious focal point readable at 320x180
- "Info gap" — creates genuine curiosity or visual tension
- Reads INSTANTLY — no squinting, no confusion
Score: 3=would stop YOUR scroll, 2=noticeable, 1=blends in, 0=invisible

**2. EMOTIONAL HOOK (0-2 points)** - Proven CTR driver
- Visible emotion/expression if face present (curiosity, awe, concern — NOT fake shock)
- OR mysterious/dramatic scene that raises questions
- Scale contrast (tiny human vs. something massive) = instant drama
- Creates "I need to know" feeling in 1 second
Score: 2=strong curiosity, 1=mild interest, 0=no hook

**3. VISUAL QUALITY (0-2 points)** - Premium, not AI-looking
- Cinematic lighting with clear direction (not flat or generic)
- Cohesive color palette (2-3 bold colors, not rainbow neon)
- Atmosphere and texture (grain, fog, depth of field — not sterile digital)
- Avoids AI tells: no perfect symmetry, no plastic skin, no floating objects
Score: 2=could be real photography/cinema, 1=decent, 0=obviously AI-generated

**4. COMPOSITION (0-2 points)**
- ONE clear focal point — not cluttered with competing elements
- High contrast between subject and background — separation is everything
- Dynamic composition (rule of thirds, diagonals, asymmetry)
- Subject fills enough of frame to read at thumbnail size
Score: 2=masterful, 1=acceptable, 0=cluttered or generic

**5. THUMBNAIL-SPECIFIC (0-1 point)**
- Works at actual YouTube size (320x180px), not just full-screen
- Distinct from similar videos (not generic stock)
- Clear visual story without needing the title
Score: 1=works perfectly small, 0=loses impact

**SCORE 0-10**.
- 3-5: Typical AI output (cluttered, neon, generic)
- 6-7: Decent but could be bolder or more refined
- 8-9: Excellent — bold AND premium
- 10: Would genuinely outperform on YouTube

**RED FLAGS (auto-deduct 2 points each):**
- Low contrast / muted colors that disappear at thumbnail size
- Multiple competing focal points (cluttered)
- Clickbait shock faces or childish imagery
- Perfect digital symmetry or plastic skin (AI tells)
- Random neon rainbow palette (AI tell)
